Y’ELLO! APPLICATION PRIVACY POLICY

What this Privacy Policy Covers

Welcome to the Y’ello! Application (the “App”). This privacy policy covers the processing of your information on the the “App”, owned and operated by Lantas Technologies, Inc. (“Lantas”).

Our primary goal in collection of the information described below is to enhance your experience on the App. We hope you enjoy using it!

Gathering of Information

Voluntary Submission

This App does not collect personally identifiable information (“PII”) about any individual App user except when knowingly and expressly provided by that individual. For example, we may ask you for information, such as your e-mail address, phone number and name when you create a login. You may also provide information to the App in other contexts, including, for example, to enter into a sweepstakes or promotion. You always have the option not to provide the information we request. However, if you choose not to provide the information we request, you may not be able to use the App or portions of the App.  When you register on or provide information on the App in any manner, you agree to provide only true, accurate, current and complete information.

You may, during your interaction with the App, submit content (such as tags, lists or groups). You agree that you are providing the content voluntarily to Lantas. The content submitted by you, may be retained by us indefinitely. If you don’t want your content to reside with Lantas, please do not post it.

Information Collected Automatically:

We automatically receive, record and store the following information from your mobile device when you interact with the Services:

Location: Your device reports and we collect information on your precise geolocation through your mobile device so you can use our location based features.  Location includes the mobile device’s location coordinates.  Such information is not treated as PII, except where we are required to do otherwise under applicable law. Your consent is required for this App to use your geolocation information. Should you wish to opt-out of this type of data collection, you may do so by changing the preferences or settings on your mobile device.

Directory Data: We use the personal directory data on your device to enhance your user experience on the App and solely for Lantas’ own internal purposes (for instance, to contact you). We do not share this data with any third party. Directory data includes, but is not limited to your address book and calendar functions. By downloading the App, you consent to our use of this data internally. You may always revoke your consent to our ongoing ability to collect information from your device’s phonebook by changing the settings on your device if your device offers that option. You can also delete the App entirely, but we hope you’ll never do that!

Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: A cookie is a small data file that is written to your computer's hard drive by your web browser. A cookie file can contain information, such as a user identification code that a website or app will use to track the pages and number of times you have visited. Lantas may use cookies to track web analytics throughout the App on browsers therein and account status so that you don't have to login each time you visit the App. We also use unique device identifiers to collect information about your device such as the model and operating system. This helps us in case we detect a crash or other problem with the App. This info is also a part the geolocation data described above. The data read from these cookies or other technologies may be linked to PII and non-PII information. Business partners of Lantas, vendors providing hosting and other services for the App and Lantas’’ third party analytics providers may set their own unique cookies or other technologies through the App.

Log Files: Lantas’ servers may log and store information about your use of the App for our own internal purposes.

Usage: By using the App, you are providing Lantas with information on your activity with your contacts and with other users of the App. We collect information on what you are doing and how you are using the App (as well as other users of the App) to provide a better experience for everyone.

To Provide Communications From Third Parties

We respect the privacy of our users and shall not disclose or distribute our user list to any third party, nor shall we permit anyone else to do so.

Control of Your Information

You may edit your account information at any time by logging on to the App and changing it there.

We will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Sharing of Information

When you are on an area of the App and are asked for personal information, you are sharing that information with Lantas as well as business partners and vendors providing hosting and other services for Lantas. This data is anonymized or de-identified.

In The Event of Sale or Bankruptcy

The ownership of Lantas may change at some point in the future. Should that occur, we want this App to be able to maintain a relationship with you. In the event of a sale, merger, public offering, bankruptcy or other change in control of Lantas, your information may be shared with the person or business that owns or controls this site. Opting out of receiving information from third parties will not affect our right to transfer your information to a new owner, but your choices will continue to be respected regarding the use of your information. You may be notified as described below in "How will I know if the Privacy Policy is changed."

As Required By Law

Lantas may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to conform to the edicts of the law or comply with a judicial proceeding, court order or legal process served on Lantas or to protect and defend the rights or property of Lantas.

Security

Your information is stored on Lantas’ servers located in the United States. We use industry standard procedural and technical safeguards to protect your personal information against loss or theft as well as unauthorized access and disclosure to protect your privacy, including encryption, "firewalls" and the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL). We treat data as an asset that must be protected against loss and unauthorized access. We employ many different security techniques to protect such data from unauthorized access by users inside and outside the company. However, "perfect security" does not exist on the Internet.

Privacy of Children

This App is presented to you through an exclusive invitation from Lantas. We do not send the invitation to and we do not knowingly collect PII from children under 13. In the event that we learn we have collected any personal information from a child under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information from our database as quickly as possible.

Account Deletion

Please contact Lantas should you want to delete your account. We can’t control the deletion of anything you post on your social media or our social media (if it is subsequently shared throughout the Internet).
